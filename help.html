Quick Tutorials

1

DISC Assessment

  • Go to the Dashboard and click Start Assessment.
  • Answer all questions honestly to discover your personality type.
  • Your results will automatically save to your profile.
2

Career Recommendations

  • Based on your assessment, PersonaPath suggests top career paths.
  • Visit Test Results to Career Recommendations to view your top-rated careers.
  • Click any career card for more insights and guidance.
3

Resume Generator

  • Navigate to Resume Generator from the sidebar.
  • Fill in each section (Basic Info to Education to Experience to Projects to Skills).
  • Click Download Resume to save your personalized professional CV.
4

Career Roadmap

  • Access the Career Roadmap page to view step-by-step paths for your selected career.
  • Each roadmap includes skills, tools, and learning suggestions to grow in your field.
5

Account Settings

  • Open Settings from the sidebar.
  • Update your profile name, email, or profile picture.
  • Your changes are saved automatically.
